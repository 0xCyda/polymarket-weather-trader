STEP 2 — Pipe stdout to format_scan.py for reformatting:
Command:
```

```
format_scan.py reformats the output with AWST timestamp, portfolio stats (balance, P&L) from paper_journal, open positions, AIFS ENS signals from the scan log, and summary (markets scanned, signals, trades).
